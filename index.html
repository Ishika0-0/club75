About Us

Welcome to CLUB~75


The CLUB75, your ultimate destination for all things related to clubs, events, and activities at Jabalpur Engineering College (JEC). As the official platform for the vibrant and dynamic community at JEC, CLUB75 aims to provide a comprehensive and centralized hub for students and alumni alike.

At CLUB75, we believe in the power of connection and collaboration. That's why we've created a platform where authorized JEC students and alumni can come together to stay informed, engaged, and connected. Whether you're looking to explore upcoming events, connect with fellow club members, or seek guidance and support, CLUB75 has you covered.

Our website features a user-friendly interface where you can easily browse through a wide range of club events and activities happening within the college. From workshops and seminars to social gatherings and competitions, you'll find all the latest happenings right here on our platform.

But CLUB~75 is more than just an events directory. It's a community-driven space where you can create your profile, connect with peers, and engage in meaningful discussions. Our chat section provides a real-time communication channel where you can ask questions, share insights, and connect with others who share your interests.

Whether you're a current student looking to get involved in campus life or an alum eager to stay connected with your alma mater, CLUB~75 welcomes you with open arms. Join us today and be a part of the vibrant JEC community!
